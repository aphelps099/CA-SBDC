NorCal SBDC Color Palette

A comprehensive guide to using the NorCal SBDC brand colors. Navy leads. Wine accents. Everything else supports.

Primary Colors

Navy

Primary brand color - Professional, trustworthy, authoritative

Navy

Proportion: 4 parts (largest)

Pool

Secondary color - Approachable, fresh, forward-thinking

Pool
#8FC5D9
Usage: Highlights, tags, secondary elements
Proportion: 2 parts

Accent Color

Wine

Strategic accent - Bold, energetic, memorable

Wine
#8A2432
Usage: Accent lines, CTAs (use sparingly!)
Proportion: 1 part (smallest, most powerful)
